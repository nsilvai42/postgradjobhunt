🧠 Psych Tip of the Week: The Goal Gradient Effect

Have you ever noticed you speed up as you get closer to the finish line? That’s the goal gradient effect—our motivation intensifies as we near a goal.

Use the extra motivation this week:

  1. Take 5 minutes to prioritize how you'll do this week's tasks.
  2. Order them by priority - most important first.
  3. Try to go through as many tasks as you can before Sprint 2 starts!

By harnessing this effect, you’ll find yourself naturally energized to push toward the finish line.
