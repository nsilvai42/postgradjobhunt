🧠 Psych Tip of the Week: Fresh Starts

Ever noticed the gym seems busier on Monday, January, and the start of a new season? Studies find we’re 30% more likely to follow through on goals we set right after a new week, month, or birthday.

These moments are called “fresh starts”: Starting strong will help you stick to your job search so don’t let this fresh start go to waste!

Every Monday morning, try taking 10 minutes to:

  • Review the week's goals and any overdue tasks in your Planner.
  • Plan when you’ll do each task using the time blocks you set aside to work on your job search.
  • Add a note to calendar events with the tasks you plan to complete during each workblock.
